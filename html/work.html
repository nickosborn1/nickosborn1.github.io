NotesGPT

NotesGPT is a mobile app AI-assistant that helps you generate and learn from your notes by transcribing audio to searchable text, summarizing notes/meetings using ChatGPT within app, and provideing an in-app ChatGPT interface so you can ask questions and dialogue about your notes/recordings. I helped develop this within a six-person team over the course of a semester. Watch this video to see how it works!
